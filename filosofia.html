Filosofía

Párrafo explicando mi producción filosósifca: interés por el realismo especulativo y el dinero, llevándolo a una filosofía de la blockchain y las criptomonedas que profundice en su realidad. Mi trabajo busca avanzar de la crítica hacia la especulación y, con ello, desatar el poder del pensamiento para estar a la altura de nuestors tiempos

Numismática Especulativa: Ontología Orientada a Monedas

This presentation seeks to converge Ole Bjerg’s psychoanalysis of money with Object Oriented Ontology in order to demonstrate that the speculative powers of money are not defined by a lack, but by a reality surplus that cryptocurrency is currently enacting. The NFT showcases the presentation and essay, with original images and diagrams made by myself. Puedes ver la presentación en el siguiente video.

The Terraforming of Bitcoin: a Speculation on Viable Planetarity

I wrote this brief text regarding Bitcoin mining and its planetarity possibilities to enter a writing contest in the HackerNoon portal. While I only received a finalist mention, it was a great opportunity to adapt Benjamin Bratton’s ideas on Terraforming; specifically, to the possibility of crypto mining assisting green energy development as an economic incentive. In this sense, financial media could be subordinate to the ecological infrastructure we need to develop for a viable planetarity.

A Tale of Two Coins

Co-autoría con Emma Baizabal

This essay was our (Emma Baizabal and I) first NFT in english. It inaugurates our interest in the future of money through blockchain technology, with a proposal of understanding money as information and the future this entails.


Uncut Coins. Money Neutrality and Reality Surplus

This presentation was a first attempt at using the Object-Oriented Ontology framework to explain an episode in monetary history: Keynes’ Theory of Money in response to the 1929 depression. I merge an ontological and technological reading of money; this is, a reading that emphasizes how money is an object with many possibilities and that such possibilities can change the course of an economy.
