Descripcion de mi acercamiento a Technical Writing: origen del interes, utilidad por su tecnologia, trabajo colaborativo


Guia de Inmersion a Hic et Nunc (HEN)

When I first entered the world of crypto, I was fascinated not only by its technological complexity and possibilities, but about the lack of information and guides in my native language. Attempting to close that gap, I created this guide to instruct spanish-speaking parties interested in NFTs on how to buy the cryptocurrency Tezos to use in the community-managed Hic et Nunc Marketplace (now Teia). The guide was my first attempt at technical writing, and it was widely used by people from Latin America, using only informal distribution channels.

Video documentacion del Seminario de Filosofia de la Economia

During my coordination of the Philosophy of Economics seminar, I took the initiative to document our talks on Youtube. Although my colleagues were already using Facebook video, it was very unreliable and difficult to search for specific talks. In these videos I did some basic editing, added the openings and closings of the videos, and established the thumbnail format for a more attractive youtube channel experience.

Confeccionar Planetas: el Metaverso de Hic et Nunc

A report on the efforts of the Hic et Nunc community to build a metaverse integrated with the NFT marketplace. This report was made through interviews with the metaverse builders and documentation research on the Hic et Nunc discord.
